rare waves - hydronium DIY

The Rare Waves Hydronium is a semi-modular synthesizer based on ATmega328P microcontroller and it's available as DIY Kit. Its tone is based on a single VCO, a resonant low-pass filter, and a simple decay envelope. It can be played either by MIDI control or CV-Gate. In addition, its built-in MIDI-to-CV converter with post-glide v/oct output lets you use it as a bridge between your MIDI controller and other analog synthesizers.
