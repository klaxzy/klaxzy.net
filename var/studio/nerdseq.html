modular gear list

XOR Electronics Nerdseq is a tracker based cv and trigger sequencer module (32HP) for Eurorack systems. It consists 6 tracks for the CV/Trigger/MOD generation of 18 outputs. 4 User assignable inputs, clock and reset in/outputs and 2 x 2 audio tracks to sequence samples and the integrated oscillators and FM synthesis.
